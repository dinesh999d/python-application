How can I check my account balance?
You can check your account balance through mobile banking, ATM, SMS banking, or internet banking.

What is the minimum balance required?
The minimum balance required for a savings account is ₹10,000. Penalty applies if not maintained.

How to reset ATM PIN?
You can reset your ATM PIN at any ATM or through internet banking.

How to block my debit card?
You can immediately block your debit card via mobile banking or by calling customer care.

How to apply for personal loan?
You can apply for a personal loan online or at the nearest branch. Interest starts from 10.5% annually.

What is the personal loan interest rate?
Personal loan interest rate starts from 10.5% annually depending on eligibility.

What is the transaction limit per day?
The maximum daily transaction limit is ₹1,00,000.

Money debited but transaction failed?
If money is debited but the transaction failed, it will be refunded within 3–5 working days.

How to download bank statement?
You can download your account statement from internet banking or the mobile app under the statements section.

How to open a new account?
You can open a new savings account online or by visiting the branch with valid ID proof.

How to close my bank account?
To close your bank account, please visit the branch and submit an account closure form.

How to update mobile number?
You can update your registered mobile number via ATM, internet banking, or by visiting the branch.

How to update address?
Address can be updated through internet banking or by submitting address proof at the branch.

How to update KYC?
You can update KYC by submitting Aadhaar and PAN at branch or through video KYC process.

How to add nominee?
Nominee details can be added or updated through internet banking or at the branch.

What are the available branches?
Branches are available in Bangalore (MG Road), Hyderabad (Banjara Hills), and Chennai (T Nagar).

What is customer care number?
Customer support number is 1800-123-456 and available 24/7.

How to apply for credit card?
You can apply for a credit card through our website. Approval depends on income and credit history.

What is card limit?
Card limit depends on your income profile and credit score.

How to enable international transactions?
You can enable international transactions from mobile banking under card settings.

How to request cheque book?
Cheque book can be requested via internet banking or branch visit.

What is FD (Fixed Deposit)?
Fixed Deposit allows you to invest a lump sum amount for a fixed tenure and earn interest.

What is RD (Recurring Deposit)?
Recurring Deposit allows you to deposit a fixed amount monthly and earn interest.

What is EMI?
EMI is the fixed monthly payment towards your loan based on tenure and interest rate.

How to foreclose loan?
Loan foreclosure can be requested at the branch. Charges may apply as per policy.

What happens if minimum balance is not maintained?
Penalty charges will be deducted if minimum balance is not maintained.

Account is blocked?
If your account is blocked, please contact customer support or visit your branch for verification.
